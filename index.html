Questions

What is Beat My Funnel? +
A pay-for-performance marketplace where brands post funnel challenges with real money in escrow. CRO experts, copywriters, and funnel builders compete to beat the brand's current metrics. If someone beats your numbers, they get paid. If nobody does, you get your money back.
How do challenges work? +
A brand defines their metric (conversion rate, ROAS, CPA, etc.), sets a prize amount, and deposits funds into escrow. Top operators submit their version. The brand reviews entries before anything goes live. Results are verified via Shopify or GA4 data. If someone beats the metric, the money transfers automatically. If nobody does, the brand gets a full refund.
How does escrow work? +
When you post a challenge, your prize money is deposited into escrow and locked until the challenge resolves. If an operator beats your metric, the funds transfer to them automatically. If nobody beats it, you get a full refund. No exceptions.
How are results verified? +
Results are verified directly through your analytics platform — Shopify, GA4, or other connected data sources. No screenshots, no self-reporting. The data speaks for itself.
What types of challenges can I post? +
Landing page and funnel conversion rate, ad creative CPA or ROAS, email open rate and click rate, homepage add-to-cart rate, and pricing page checkout conversion. Every challenge has a preset win condition defined before anyone starts.
Is it free? +
Free to join during the private beta. Brands only pay the challenge prize if someone beats their metrics. Operators compete for free and get paid when they win.
