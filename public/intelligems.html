FAQ

Everything you need to know

How challenges work, how verification works, and what makes this different.

What is Beat My Funnel? +
A performance marketplace where brands post challenges with real money in escrow. Operators compete to beat the brand's current metrics. Results are verified through concurrent A/B tests powered by Intelligems. Winners get paid automatically.
How does Intelligems fit in? +
Intelligems is the testing and verification layer. Every challenge runs as a concurrent split test through Intelligems — real-time traffic splitting, revenue per visitor tracking, and automated statistical significance. Both the brand and the operator see the same live dashboard. No screenshots, no self-reporting.
How do challenges work? +
Brand defines a metric and current baseline (e.g. 3.2% CVR). Sets a prize and deposits it into escrow. Operators apply and submit their version. The winning submission runs as a concurrent A/B test through Intelligems. If someone beats the baseline with statistical significance, the prize releases automatically.
How does escrow work? +
Funds are held by the platform from the moment a challenge goes live. The brand cannot withdraw them during the challenge window. If nobody beats the baseline, the full prize is refunded. If someone wins, the prize transfers automatically upon verification.
Why concurrent A/B testing? +
Sequential testing introduces timing bias — traffic quality changes by day, by season, by campaign. Concurrent testing through Intelligems splits the same traffic pool in real time, ensuring a fair comparison. It's the only way to get a trustworthy result.
What types of challenges can be tested? +
Landing pages, product pages, pricing strategies, offer and discount structures, checkout flows, shipping thresholds — anything Intelligems can split test on Shopify. The Intelligems integration dramatically expands what's possible beyond just page-level CVR.
How are results verified? +
Through Intelligems directly. The platform tracks revenue per visitor, conversion rate, and other metrics in real time. Statistical significance is calculated automatically. Both parties see the same live dashboard — there's nothing to dispute.
Who owns the submissions? +
Brands only acquire the rights to the winning submission. Non-winning submissions remain the intellectual property of the operator. Brands may not use elements of non-winning submissions without purchasing them directly from the operator.
